We are recruiting organizing members. Please apply through our [Organizing Member Application Form](https://forms.gle/7irqYKhZVj7AY7LfA).

## 18 Days Until the Event

Today is September 8th, and we have 18 days remaining until PyCon JP 2025.

We've entered the final preparation phase, with all teams intensifying their activities. This period focuses on detailed coordination and confirmation work, while also being a time when new challenges become visible.

## Today's Main Activities

### Chair Team Activities

- Confirming coordination between teams
- Identifying challenges in the final stage
- Final adjustments to the event day operations structure

### Overall Progress Status

At this 18-day mark, each team is in the following situation:

- **Program Team**: Final confirmation of presentation content
- **Venue Team**: Detailed planning for setup
- **Sponsor Team**: Preparing for event day support
- **Attendee Management Team**: Setting up reception and guidance systems
- **PR Team**: Preparing final announcements

## This Week's Priority Issues

### 1. Strengthening Inter-team Coordination

We're entering the stage of integrating the preparations that each team has been working on independently. Coordination between teams will be particularly important for event day operations.

### 2. Final Information for Participants

We're preparing final information for participants, including venue access, event day schedule, and important notices.

### 3. Emergency Response Preparation

Confirming response systems for unexpected situations is also a crucial task.

## To All Organizing Members

The 18-day mark represents the final phase of our preparations. This is when our preparation efforts bear fruit, while also being a time for final adjustments.

### What We'd Like to Ask

1. **Share Anxieties and Concerns**: Please don't hesitate to share even small concerns
2. **Inter-team Information Sharing**: If you have information relevant to other teams, please share it actively
3. **Health Management**: Especially in this final stage, please take care of your health without overexerting

## About the Hiroshima Event

With just 18 days until our event at Hiroshima International Conference Center, coordination with local Hiroshima members is progressing smoothly, and we have a strong feeling this will be an excellent event.

We're making final preparations to ensure that participants coming from afar can also experience the charm of Hiroshima.

## Upcoming Schedule

- Next week: Final confirmation meetings for each team
- Week after next: On-site preparation begins
- Event week: Final adjustments and event day operations

## As Chair

At this 18-day mark, I'm feeling a mix of nervousness and excitement. The results of our preparations with many organizing members are about to take shape.

Let's continue without letting our guard down, but also without overexerting ourselves, as we all work together to create an excellent PyCon JP 2025.
